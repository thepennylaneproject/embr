## Core Principles in These Mockups

1. **Whitespace > Cards** - Section breaks use spacing, not boxes
2. **Typography > Color** - Hierarchy through size, not color
3. **Numbers > Metrics** - Show actual values, not engagement counts
4. **Clarity > Engagement** - Obvious what to do, not what keeps you scrolling
5. **Transparency > Mystery** - See the money, see the decisions, see the people
6. **Creator Voice > Platform Voice** - Creators tell their story, not "featured" by algorithm
7. **Collective > Individual** - Groups are visible, not hidden in DMs
8. **Support > Follow** - The button is "Subscribe" or "Tip", not "Follow"

## What You DON'T See

❌ Follower counts
❌ Like/share/comment counts
❌ Trending algorithms
❌ Recommended posts
❌ Engagement notifications
❌ Leaderboards
❌ Badges or gamification
❌ Ads or sponsored content
❌ Multiple call-to-actions per section
❌ Animations or visual fluff

---

## Implementation Notes

- Use CSS Grid and Flexbox (not Bootstrap cards)
- Max container width: 900px (narrow, readable)
- Base font: 18px body (readable)
- Line height: 1.6 (breathing room)
- Margins: 16/24/32/48/64px (spacious)
- Colors: 3-4 total (black, grays, one accent)
- No shadows (use borders or background)
- No rounded corners > 12px (clean, modern)
